# Five Flows of the ESS Industrial Cell

> Every club adhesion activates 5 coordinated flows. None works in isolation, and together they form the redistribution loop.

Version: v2
Date: 2026-04-19

## Flow 1, CROWNIUM SAS franchise

The existing club association signs a franchise agreement with CROWNIUM. The club operates under a SAS structure aligned with the mutualist capital architecture. No equity transfer to external speculators. Inspired by Bundesliga 50+1.

Role: central engine of the cell. Provides governance framework, revenue stream structuring, and regulatory compatibility with UEFA Squad Cost Rule.

## Flow 2, SCIC supporters

A cooperative society of collective interest (SCIC) is formed by interested supporters. The SCIC holds a minority stake in the club SAS and receives mutualized micro-dividends.

Role: non-dilutive capital injection, affective anchoring, reputational retention. Supporters become co-owners of the capital flow without becoming shareholders of the sporting operation.

## Flow 3, COLHYBRI merchant tissue

Local merchants in the catchment area are onboarded onto COLHYBRI, the SaaS platform digitalizing the Neapolitan cafe sospeso tradition.

Role: merchant tissue. Creates a 2.5x multiplier effect on local spend when deployed in tandem with CROWNIUM. Channels solidarity into pseudonymized, dignified beneficiary access.

## Flow 4, ONLYMORE FINANCE intragroup treasury

ONLYMORE FINANCE opens and operates the intragroup treasury account, activating the L.511-7 CMF exemption for cash circulation between holding and the new cell. Lombard credit structuring for working capital needs.

Role: banking interface. Enables liquidity routing without banking license. Positions the cell on patient capital horizons (7-10 years).

## Flow 5, DOJUKU SHINGI practice base

The AI-driven martial arts platform launches in the territory, with intergenerational pedagogy and the SHINGAN notation system.

Role: non-sports revenue loop. Deepens community attachment beyond the sports club, engages youth and seniors, produces IP content for PLUMAYA Editions.

## Coordination

The 5 flows are not independent. A supporter in flow 2 typically also subscribes to COLHYBRI merchants in flow 3. A COLHYBRI merchant typically sponsors the club in flow 1. A DOJUKU student's family typically attends club events in flow 1.

The cell measures this coordination as network density: number of cross-flow links per resident. A mature cell exceeds 2.0 cross-flow links per adult resident within 18 months.

## Measurement

Monthly KPI pack includes:
- Flow 1: SAS franchise revenues, SCIC dividend distributions
- Flow 2: SCIC supporter count, reputational NPS
- Flow 3: COLHYBRI merchant count, active, tier distribution, suspended items served
- Flow 4: Treasury flow volume, working capital utilization
- Flow 5: DOJUKU student count, lesson hours, SHINGAN notation events

Annual report aggregates the 5 flows into the 4 canonical outputs (jobs, merchant revenues, supporter dividends, sports licenses).
