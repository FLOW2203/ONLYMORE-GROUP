# ESS Industrial Cell, deployment playbook

> Operational unit of ONLYMORE Group. Deployed on a given territory within 6 weeks.

Version: v2
Date: 2026-04-19

## Definition

The cellule ESS industriel (ESS industrial cell) is the unit of production of ONLYMORE Group. It is not a company, not a product, not a service. It is an operational assembly of five coordinated flows that, once activated, rebuild a local redistribution loop around a sports institution.

## Six-week deployment

### Week 1, territorial diagnostic
Mapping of the existing club, local merchant network, administrative boundaries, regulatory context, and stakeholder network. A deployment playbook is customized.

### Week 2, club SAS franchise
The existing club association signs the CROWNIUM SAS franchise agreement. The club retains majority voting rights per Bundesliga 50+1 inspiration.

### Week 3, SCIC formation
Interested supporters form a cooperative society of collective interest (SCIC) that will receive a minority stake and mutualized micro-dividends. Registration via standard Greffe procedures, 1-2 weeks.

### Week 4, COLHYBRI merchant onboarding
First cohort of 20-40 merchants in the catchment area is trained and onboarded onto the three tiers (Basic 3 EUR, Standard 10 EUR, Premium 15 EUR per month).

### Week 5, ONLYMORE FINANCE interface
ONLYMORE FINANCE opens the intragroup treasury account, activating the L.511-7 CMF exemption for cash circulation between holding and the new cell.

### Week 6, DOJUKU launch
The martial arts practice base opens with the AI-driven application deployed to initial users. Intergenerational pedagogy starts.

## Measurable outputs, monthly reporting

- Direct jobs created
- Indirect jobs supported in the merchant tissue
- Merchant revenues net uplift
- Supporter dividends distributed
- Sports licenses funded (amateur + pro)

## Regulatory envelope

- UEFA Squad Cost Rule (70 percent cap on squad costs)
- Bundesliga 50+1 (club majority control retained)
- ESS France + ESUS approval (three criteria: primacy, democracy, reinvestment)
- L.511-7 CMF (intragroup treasury exemption)
- EU Resolution P10_TA(2025)0212 (mutualist model recognition)
- IOBSP ORIAS (banking intermediation registration Q1 2027)

## What the cell is not

It is not a startup accelerator, not a sports investment fund, not an ESG certification. It is an infrastructure that, once deployed, continues operating autonomously. The deployment is the product. The reporting is the proof.
